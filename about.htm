I wished to get more training in the computational field. Digital Humanities (MA) program at the University of Alberta seemed a sound opportunity to enhance my skills and, possibly, sligtly change the focus of my career. Indeed, I benefited from courses in Web Design, Python, PHP, Project Management and lots of theoretical courses on human-computer interaction, AI and computational methods in humanities.

We had a unique experience working with real oraganizations and communicating with stakeholders directly.

This experience inspired me to build a career as a UI/UX designer as, I believe, this area allows me to apply my journalistic and linguistic background as well as technical skills. I like being people-oriented and create products that adress people's feelings.
